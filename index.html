About Interactive Media Design

The Interactive Media Design course offers an in-depth exploration of the multifaceted world of digital experiences, where creativity and technology converge to shape how users engage with content across various platforms. Throughout the semester, students will immerse themselves in the fundamental principles and practices that underpin effective interactive design, delving into a wide array of media forms such as websites, mobile applications, video games, and immersive installations. This course is structured to facilitate a hands-on approach to learning, encouraging students to engage in a series of collaborative workshops and individual projects that emphasize the importance of user experience (UX) design and interface design.
